REAL-TIME GEOPOLITICAL INTELLIGENCE

This project is supported by the Program Management Unit for Area Based Development (PMU A) and the Digital Economy Promotion Agency (depa).

Created by Non Arkaraprasertkul — architect, urban designer, and smart city specialist; Harvard-affiliated doctoral researcher in anthropology and cities focused on human-centered smart cities and real-world implementation — and Associate Professor Poon Thiengburanathum as a public ranking model designed to explore alternative ways of understanding urban performance.

Their work sits at the intersection of urban design, data, and human behavior, bringing a distinctly people-centered perspective to how cities are measured and experienced.
